About Us

If the color scheme and pictures did not already make it obvious or maybe you're a newcomer to the world of Hoxxes IV, this page is dedicated to the driller class in Deep Rock Galactic. I myself am a big fan of the Driller, from the playstyle to the customizations this class was made for me. I hope after exploring this page you will feel the class is superior to the rest of the classes and find a love for the rugged dwarf known as the Driller.

About Deep Rock Galactic

Deep Rock Galactic is a game about dwarves and mining. The game is set in space and the player(s) (dwarves) must complete missions assigned to them by their employer, Deep Rock Galactic. The missions span from a wide variety of tasks from simply collecting morkite in a tame cave system to bulldozing your way through enemies to stop rival companies from accomplishing their goals. Although the game is chaotic and you never know what to expect, one thing will always remain true. Rock and Stone!

There are 4 playable classes to choose from:

  • The Engineer
  • The Scout
  • The Gunner
  • The DRILLER

About the Driller

The driller is one of the four playable classes in the beloved video game Deep Rock Galactic. Personally my favorite class because who doesn't like digging? I would recommend this class to players who love destruction and chaos since the Driller is equipped with mines, a flamethrower, and a big ass drill. Alternatively you could use throwing axes, a variety of grenades, and a cryo cannon (ice flamethrower).

What makes the Driller so fun?

The driller makes an excellent class for players who like making their own path, literally. Is there no clear way to get to some nitra at the top of the cave? Just dig your way up there! The Driller doesn't allow anything to stop them and if you like the sound of that then you need to try the class. There are so many possible builds and the ability to just dig anywhere you want allows for so much creativity. Not only is the Driller excellent for solo play but they are the beating heart of any team. When there is a thick wall of rock and stone in the cave and your team needs to get to the other side, who is everyone going to turn to? The Driller of course!

They're always there for you

Finally, I would like to add that the Driller is the ultimate team player. There is a saying in Deep Rock Galactic, "No dwarf left behind". Of course we would all like to make it to the escape pod at the end of the mission, but this can be a troubling and intense moment due to the complexity of the cave system. I would like to take this time to remind the engineers and gunners out there, that when that stinking scout goes grappling across the cave and leaving us in their dust. The Driller will always be there to help carve a safe path for their team, like a true hero.
